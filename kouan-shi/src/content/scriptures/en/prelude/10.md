# Discarding Attachment, Standing Imperturbable

<br>

## True Meaning
### If you cast away the fear of loss, you shall stand alone between heaven and earth, dwelling within an unwavering silence.

<br>

## Interpretation
#### The essence of humanity is "nothingness"; essentially, there is nothing to be lost.
#### To "be" is, in itself, the supreme happiness.
#### Reconfirm the state of "existence," and find joy in the truth that it still "exists" even now.
#### Tranquility arrives at the very moment attachment is discarded.
