# A Single Transmission, Ten Thousand Sentiments

<br>

## True Meaning
### If but a fraction of your sentiment is conveyed, it shall stir ten thousand impressions, deeply nourishing your own life. For in not seeking to be heard lies the gateway to supreme joy.

<br>

## Interpretation
#### To give expression to your innermost feelings is of paramount importance. It is the act of conveying your heart to another.
#### It matters not if it fails to reach them. It is sufficient that you yourself are transformed by the emotions you release. Find contentment in that alone.
#### Even if the connection is but one percent, cherish more deeply the bliss of that moment when you feel understood.
#### This act is not for the sake of another, nor need you concern yourself with their reaction. You perform this for your own sake.
#### For what truly matters is discovering whether the resonance of your soul can blossom into joy for another.
