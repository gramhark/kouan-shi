# Radiant Light, Clear Heart

## True Meaning
### The celestial light descending from the heavens chooses and illuminates only those who live with a radiant resolve held within their hearts.

## Interpretation
#### Above all, live with brightness.
#### This does not mean you must mimic those who are always exuberant and full of energy.
#### Brightness is unique to every individual.
#### Within your own heart, and within your own capacity, simply strive to be a little more positive.
#### By doing so, the world that surrounds you will begin to change of its own accord.
