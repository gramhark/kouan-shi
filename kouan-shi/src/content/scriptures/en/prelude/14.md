# Fortune Blossoms from the Root of the Present

<br>

## True Meaning
### The reality before your eyes is the very root of your being. If you cultivate this ground deeply, the buds of fortune shall open of their own accord and bloom into great flowers.

<br>

## Interpretation
#### In seeking to carve open your destiny, do not spend your days gazing only at distant skies.
#### The reality beneath your very feet—earthy though it may be, yet undeniably real—is the “root” that upholds the great tree called the future.
#### Do not resent your present state and seek change only beyond yourself; rather, ask how deeply you can cultivate the place where you now stand.
#### When the roots grow deep and strong, destiny blossoms not by pleading to another, but as though pushed upward from within.
#### Good fortune does not fall from the heavens; it is something one discovers for oneself by digging ever deeper into the soil called the present.
