# Fulfilling Intent by Responding to the Now

<br>

## True Meaning
### Regret not the time that has passed, nor fear the time yet to come. The resolve you embrace shall swiftly lose its heat if it comes to a halt. Contemplate only the present, and let it build upon itself.

<br>

## Interpretation
#### Do not regret the time that has passed. Do not worry about the time that has yet to arrive.
#### Think only of the self who exists here and now; do not think of the self who is absent from this moment.
#### An ambition held loses its fire the more it is allowed to stagnate.
#### By acting in response to the "now," your history is etched and your future is forged.
