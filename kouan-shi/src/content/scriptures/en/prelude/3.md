# Returning to Void, Unbound Heart

<br>

## True Meaning
### As it is the fate of all creation to perish, one ought to simply let one's heart revel in the solitary fact of one's own existence.

<br>

## Interpretation
#### Come what may, all things will eventually fade away.
#### Since they are destined to vanish, release the worldly distractions that bind your heart, here and now.
#### Instead, contemplate the miracle that you simply "exist" here at this moment.
#### Do not surrender the precious time remaining until your most cherished "self" fades away to anyone else.
